Irish Folk Bands

The Pogues

The Pogues were a celtic punk band fronted by Shane MacGowan and others, founded in Kings Cross, London in 1982.

The band reached international prominece in the 1980s and early 1990s, recording several hit albums and singles.

The Dubliners

The Dubliners were an Irish folk band founded in 1962.

The band garnered international success with their lively Irish folk songs, traditional street ballads and instrumentals.
